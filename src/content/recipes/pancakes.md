## Description

These pancakes are super fluffy and quick to make.

## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cups flour
* 1 tbsp sugar
* 1 egg
* 1 1/4 cups milk

## Instructions

1. Mix dry ingredients then add wet ingredients.
2. Cook on a hot griddle for 2-3 minutes per side.
