## Description

An easy vegetable stir-fry full of color and flavor.

## Ingredients

* Mixed vegetables
* Soy sauce
* Garlic
* Sesame oil

## Instructions

1. Heat wok with oil, add garlic and vegetables.
2. Stir-fry 5-7 minutes and add sauce.
